# DY-Threads — Setup Guide
## Your live URL: https://yuvacka123.github.io/DY-Threads-Service

---

## Step 1 — Upload files to your repo
1. Go to: https://github.com/yuvacka123/DY-Threads-Service
2. Click "uploading an existing file" (or "Add file" → "Upload files")
3. Drag and drop ALL files from this zip:
   - index.html
   - manifest.json
   - sw.js
   - icons/ folder (with icon-192.png and icon-512.png)
4. Click "Commit changes"

---

## Step 2 — Enable GitHub Pages
1. In your repo, click the Settings tab
2. Click "Pages" in the left sidebar
3. Under Source → select "Deploy from a branch"
4. Branch: main | Folder: / (root)
5. Click Save
6. Wait 2–3 minutes

Your app is live at:
https://yuvacka123.github.io/DY-Threads-Service

---

## Step 3 — Install on iPhone
1. Open Safari on your iPhone
2. Go to: https://yuvacka123.github.io/DY-Threads-Service
3. Tap the Share button (□↑) at the bottom
4. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
5. Name it: DY-Threads
6. Tap Add

✅ Opens fullscreen like a native app — no browser bar!

---

## Step 4 — Install on Android
1. Open Chrome on your Android
2. Go to: https://yuvacka123.github.io/DY-Threads-Service
3. Tap 3-dot menu → "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
4. Tap Add

✅ Works offline too!

---

## Backup your data
Settings tab → "Export orders" → save JSON to Google Drive weekly.
