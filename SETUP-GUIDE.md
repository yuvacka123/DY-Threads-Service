# Darzi Studio — Setup Guide
## How to get the app on your iPhone & Android (Free)

---

## What you'll need
- A free GitHub account (github.com)
- The 3 files: index.html, manifest.json, sw.js (+ icons folder)
- 10–15 minutes

---

## Step 1 — Create a free GitHub account
1. Go to **github.com**
2. Click "Sign up" and create a free account
3. Verify your email

---

## Step 2 — Create a new repository
1. Click the **+** icon (top right) → "New repository"
2. Name it: `darzi-studio`
3. Set it to **Public**
4. Click **"Create repository"**

---

## Step 3 — Upload your files
1. Click **"uploading an existing file"** link on the new repo page
2. Drag and drop ALL these files/folders:
   - `index.html`
   - `manifest.json`
   - `sw.js`
   - `icons/` folder (with icon-192.png and icon-512.png)
3. Click **"Commit changes"**

---

## Step 4 — Enable GitHub Pages (makes it a live website)
1. In your repo, click **Settings** tab
2. Scroll to **"Pages"** in the left sidebar
3. Under "Source", select **"Deploy from a branch"**
4. Branch: **main** | Folder: **/ (root)**
5. Click **Save**
6. Wait 2–3 minutes. Your app URL will appear:
   `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/darzi-studio`

---

## Step 5 — Install on iPhone
1. Open Safari on your iPhone
2. Go to your app URL above
3. Tap the **Share button** (box with arrow) at the bottom
4. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Tap **"Add"**
6. ✅ Darzi Studio appears on your home screen like a real app!

---

## Step 6 — Install on Android
1. Open Chrome on your Android phone
2. Go to your app URL
3. Tap the **3-dot menu** (top right)
4. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
5. Tap **"Add"**
6. ✅ Done! Works offline too.

---

## Backup your data
- Go to the **Settings tab** in the app
- Tap **"Export all orders (JSON)"**
- Save the file to Google Drive or your phone
- Do this weekly to keep your data safe
